# Lesson Learned: GPIOA Moder bit setting had bit shift overlap
- **Context/Tags**: `gpio`, `stm32`
- **Timestamp**: 2026-05-30 13:37:32

## 🛑 Resolved Error
GPIOA Moder bit setting had bit shift overlap

## 💡 Successful Resolution
Use GPIO_MODER_MODER5_0 instead of raw 0x01 shift on Pin A5
